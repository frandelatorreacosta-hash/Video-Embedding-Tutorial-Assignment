How to Embed a Video into a Website

Step 1: Go to the video

Open YouTube and select a video you want to embed.

Step 2: Click Share

Under the video, click the Share button.

Step 3: Click Embed

Select the Embed option from the share menu.

Step 4: Copy the code

Copy the iframe embed code provided.

Step 5: Paste into HTML

Paste the iframe code into your webpage.
